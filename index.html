Americans are hungry for democracy and are turning out in record numbers to vote. But that means long lines and sometimes empty stomachs, which might discourage these brave patriots from performing their civic duty.

Fortunately Pizza to the Polls is here to deliver the one thing that pairs so perfectly with freedom: piping hot 'za. Send us reports of long lines and we'll send in the delicious reinforcements.

Report a long line

We need you to help us identify long polling lines full of hungry voters. Use this form to share a link to any tweets or instagrams that include evidence of the long lines and let us know where the polling place is.

Required: We'll make sure there's really a line.
Required: We'll verify the address using public polling place data.

Optional: So we can follow up when the pizza is inbound

Where do you get your pizza from?

We use SliceLife to find pizza places close to the lines since they let us tip and don't require someone to sign on delivery. We like to go with the local spots as much as possible.

What if there are funds left over?

Every dollar collected will go towards feeding hungry Americans. We just made this in a couple days so haven't had the time to pick out a specific charity, but if you have suggestions, please let us know!
