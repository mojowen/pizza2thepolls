COVID-19 Upate

With a heavy heart we've decided to suspend pizza deliveries to polling sites until further notice.

The risk of spreading infections with shared pizzas at crowded polling places and to delivery professionals is just too high.

We're urging folks to contact their representatives and election officials and ask for delaying in-person voting and expanding vote by mail (ask members of Congress to support the Resilient Elections During Quarantines and Natural Disasters Act).

We're also thinking about how to use our resources to help and support professionals responding to the crises and at risk folks - suggestions are welcome at ideas@polls.pizza.

Stay safe,
- Pizza to the Polls 🇺🇸 🗳 ❤️ 🍕
